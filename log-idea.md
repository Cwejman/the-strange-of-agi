# Log — Idea

Create a `log.md` as a human-readable diary of how the project developed.

## Concept

- Not a reference (that's MEMORY.md) — a diary
- Captures decisions, shifts in thinking, things that worked or didn't
- Appended mid-session whenever something notable happens, not just at the end

## How it would work

Say "append a log entry" at any point. Claude writes a short dated paragraph in plain language. No fixed format.

## Why not yet

Needs a good first entry to set the tone. Return to this when the moment feels right.
